How to create Beneficiary?

  • In the Login page, click on NewUser?Click Here to Register, to register as beneficiary.
  • In the register page,enter all the mandatory fields along with the captcha provided and submit.
  • After submitting, enter the otp which will be received to your registered mobile number.
  • if the provided otp is valid, you wil be getting message ‘OTP Verified Succesfully’,and it will be redirected to login page.

Ways to Login:

  1. Login with Username/Email/Mobile
    • Enter Username/Mobile Number/email and password along with the captcha provided to Login.
    OR
  2. Login via Otp
    • If beneficiary didn’t remind his password,he could enter his registered mobile number and wait for the otp.
    • Enter the otp which beneficiary received to his registered mobile number and login.

Forgot Password?

  • If beneficiary forgots the password,click Forgot Password? and enter registered mobile number
  • Username will be sent to you registered mobilenumber and email.

Forgot Username?

  • If Beneficiary forgot his username click Forgot Username? and enter registered mobile number.
  • Username will be sent to you registered mobilenumber and email.

How to generate the Grievance?

  • After Beneficiary Login,On the RightTop,click New Grievance button to create grievance.
  • Enter all the Required Fileds and submit grievacne.
  • Once grievance is submitted,portal will ask to give a feedback,which is optional
  • If beneficiary wants to give feedback,click on yes and select feedback form type( IPD FEEDBACK or OPD FEEDBACK )

IPD FORM

  • Select the hospital which Beneficiary wants to give feeback and submit
  • After submit,portal displays the form,please provide all required fields and submit the form.
  • Portal asks a confirmation like Are you sure want to submit IPD Form Feedback? click on yes and submit.
  • Portal displays a message fedback submitted succesfully

OPD FORM

  • Select the hospital which Beneficiary wants to give feeback and submit
  • After submit,portal displays the form,please provide all required fields and submit the form.
  • Portal asks a confirmation like Are you sure want to submit OPD Form Feedback? click on yes and submit.
  • Portal displays a message fedback submitted succesfully.

How to check the progress of Grievance?

  • In the Dashboard,Beneficiary can see grievances created by him/her.
  • By default, when Beneficiary creates the Grievance. Status is on pending.
  • If Admin Level Users, accepts the Grievance. Status wil be Accepted.
  • If Admin Level Users, closes the Grievance. Status wil be Closed.
  • If Admin Level Users, rejects the Grievance. Status wil be Rejected.
  • If Beneficiary wants to know the progress click on view button,which will show Timeline from GrievanceRegistered to Grievance closed.

How to reopen the Grievance?

  • Closed Grievance can be reopened for one time by Beneficiary.
  • Once Grievance is closed, Click on Feedback button under the Action,select Feedback as Unsatisfied and submit.
  • Status of the grievance will be reopened.
